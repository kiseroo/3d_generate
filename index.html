About 3DNexus

We're revolutionizing 3D content creation with cutting-edge AI technology

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by a team of AI researchers and 3D graphics enthusiasts, 3DNexus was born from a simple idea: make professional 3D creation accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill.

After years of research and development, we created a revolutionary AI system capable of understanding human intent and translating it into detailed 3D models. What once took professional artists days now takes seconds.

Today, our mission is to democratize 3D creation and empower creators worldwide to bring their ideas to life without barriers.

Our Journey

2021

Founded 3DNexus with a vision to make 3D creation accessible to everyone through AI technology.

2022

Released first prototype of our text-to-3D generation system, achieving breakthrough results in accuracy and detail.

2023

Expanded capabilities with image-to-3D and sketch-to-3D generation, and secured Series A funding to accelerate development.

2024

Launched our full platform with advanced editing tools and enterprise solutions, serving over 100,000 creators worldwide.

2025

Pushing the boundaries of what's possible with real-time generation and next-generation AI models.
